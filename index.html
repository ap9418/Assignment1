MY STORY

Hi, I’m Avani Patri, a freshman at NYU Abu Dhabi, intending to double major in BOS and IM. I’m passionate about art, dance, reading and most importantly— movies! I’m also very good at convincing myself that 'one more episode' or 'one more chapter' won’t hurt… until it does.

Like most students, I’ve had my fair share of procrastination. This website came out of that very habit. I wanted to explore procrastination not as a flaw, but as something almost everyone experiences and often struggles with silently. Through this project, I hope people recognize their own patterns, pause for a moment, and maybe rethink how they use their time. Procrastination is normal, but so is learning how to work around it. And if this page nudges you to start that assignment a little earlier than planned, then it’s already done its job.
